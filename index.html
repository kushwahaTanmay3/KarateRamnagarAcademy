Karate Ramnagar Academy | Coach
Mohammed Mehtab Alam

Co-Owner – Karate Ramnagar Academy Mohammed Mehtab Alam is a Black Belt 2nd Dan and a qualified National Referee in karate. He is the co-owner of Karate Ramnagar Academy and plays an important role in training students, managing competitions, and maintaining high technical standards in the academy. With strong knowledge of rules, discipline, and match experience, he supports players in improving their performance at district, state, and national levels. His dedication and guidance help students grow into confident and skilled karate athletes.

Owner & Head Coach – Karate Ramnagar Academy Mohammed Aftab Alam is a Black Belt 3rd Dan and a professional karate coach from Uttar Pradesh, India. He is the founder and head coach of Karate Ramnagar Academy, where he has been training students in discipline, self-defense, and competitive karate for many years. He is also associated with the Karate Association of Uttar Pradesh as a coach and has guided many students to perform at district, state, university, and national levels. Under his training, students have achieved remarkable success, with some players representing India at International, Asian, and World-level karate competitions. Although he has not personally traveled abroad for competitions, his dedication and coaching have helped his students reach international platforms. His mission is to build strong, confident, and disciplined players and promote karate at the grassroots level in Uttar Pradesh.
